The Garden of Forking Paths

- Capture

I went up to my bedroom. Absurd though the gesture was, I closed and locked thedoor. I threw myself down on my narrow iron bed, and waited on my back. Thenever Chaneing roortopsfled the window, and the hazy six o'clock sun hung in thethat of my implacable death.


In despite of my dead father, in despite of having been achild in one of the symmetrical gardens of Hai Feng, was I to die now?


Then I reflected that all things happen, happen to one, precisely now. Centuryfollows century, and things happen only in the present. There are countless men inthe air, on land and at sea, and all that really happens happens to me...


The almost unbearable memory of Madden's long horseface put an end to these wandering thoughts.



......



I sat up on the bed, in senseless and perfect silence, as if Madden was already peeringat me. Something - perhaps merely a desire to prove my total penury to myself -made me empty out my pockets. I found just what I knew I was going to find.


Absurdly I helt it and weighed it in my hand, to give myself courage. Vaguely I thought that a pistol shot can be heard for a great distance.
